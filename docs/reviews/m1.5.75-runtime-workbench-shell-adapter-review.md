## Confirmed Properties

- The shell adapter wraps only the W1.5.74 presenter public API.
- The adapter exposes the expected external-store shape for future React binding: `getSnapshot()`, `getServerSnapshot()`, and `subscribe(() => void)`.
- The adapter also exposes shell operation facades: `dispatch()`, `setActivePanel()`, `resolveKeyEvent()`, `handleKeyEvent()`, `listenerCount()`, `dispose()`, and `isDisposed()`.
- Public adapter objects and snapshots do not expose raw presenter, host, workbench, interaction, shortcut, controller, session, factory, adapter, viewModel, store, or stream internals.
- Snapshots are frozen and cached by signature to preserve identity when state does not change.
- Factory-created adapters are isolated and can override the factory-level `onError`.
- Listener and `onError` failures are isolated.
- Adapter dispose releases its presenter subscription, clears listeners, and disposes the adapter-owned presenter without disposing the injected host.
- Disposed adapters fail closed.
