## Residual Risk

- This remains module-level pre-Electron coverage, not real Electron `ipcMain` wiring, preload/renderer behavior, SSE/EventSource, or cross-window broadcast.
- It does not emit `system.runtime_ready` StreamEvents and does not validate native `flock` / Windows `LockFileEx`.
